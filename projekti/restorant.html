About Us

Founded in 2016

SALT is a restaurant concept developed by the REVO group, known for its expertise in hospitality, property management, marketing, and events. Salt offers a taste of Mediterranean cuisine, seafood, and sushi, catering to a wide range of dining experiences from daytime meals to nightlife and events. Our mission is to revolutionize the dining experience by exceeding client expectations, creating memorable moments with exceptional food and drink.
